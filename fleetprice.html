Pricing FAQ

What's included in the base rate?

Our base rate includes the vehicle, professional chauffeur, standard amenities (bottled water, WiFi, phone chargers), and up to 1 hour of service. Additional time is billed at the hourly rate.

Are there any additional fees?

We pride ourselves on transparent pricing. The only additional charges would be for tolls, parking fees (if applicable), and gratuity for your chauffeur (15-20% recommended).

Do you offer corporate discounts?

Yes, we offer special corporate rates for businesses with frequent transportation needs. Please contact our corporate team to discuss volume discounts and billing arrangements.

What is your cancellation policy?

Reservations can be cancelled up to 24 hours before service with no penalty. Cancellations within 24 hours are subject to a 50% charge, and within 4 hours to full charge.

How are wait times handled?

Airport pickups include 60 minutes of complimentary wait time after flight arrival. Other services include 15 minutes of wait time. Additional wait time is billed at $25 per 15 minutes.
